PXT Select is an assessment tool designed to help organizations make smarter hiring decisions.

With PXT we’ll help your organisation make smarter hires with scientifically validated assessments and actionable data, ensuring the perfect fit for every role.


A Powerful Solution That Is Simple, Human, and Smart

PXT Select helps select the right people for the job so organisations and people thrive. We help align people to roles based on the traits most important to be successful, so you can hire smarter, and retain longer. With PXT Select, hire the right people to build a better future.



One Assessment. FOUR simple Steps

1. PLAN

Know the Job


Hiring managers and recruiters are often not aligned on the job requirements prior to castng the recruiting net. Our breadth of performance models can help get everyone on the same page so they know exactly who they’re looking for, and the traits required to be successful in the role.

2. Access

KNow the Candidate


The personalised content in the profile deepens self-understanding through the DiSC model. People gain insight into their own preferences and tendencies, learn more about relating to others, and receive actionable strategies to help them improve their interactions and ultimately, their performance.

3. Choose

Know the Fit


The profile comes to life in a virtual or in-person classroom experience that engages and educates. Easy-to-use facilitation tools, including scripted content, contemporary video, and impactful activities, can be customised to meet the specific and remote needs of your people and your organisation.

4. ENGAGE

Know your People


A suite of follow-up resources makes lasting behavior change possible by keeping Everything DiSC alive in the workplace. Participants can go deeper into their DiSC style, get real-world tips for connecting with colleagues, and gain insight into their team’s or department’s DiSC culture.
